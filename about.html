About

I am an Actor, Director, Creator, Educator, Producer and most recently Web Developer. I go by she/her. I make art in the hopes that audiences will be transported to a different world, and while there be entertained, experience empathy, and be left with questions about being human. My favourite things are funny things, kind things, and interesting things.

As an actor I have performed all over Canada; in Toronto, at The Magnetic North Festival in Kitchener/Waterloo, Theatre by The Bay in Barrie, in Baddeck, Cape Breton and The Ottawa Fringe. A play I co-created and produced, Dead Roads premiered a workshop production at The RISER Project in 2016, and has continued to be developed at October Oasis at The National Arts Centre and 2016/17/18 company in residence at Factory Theatre. A film I associate produced and starred in, 9 9 . 7 % was an Official Selection at the Austin Gay and Lesbian Film Festival in 2014.

I was the Artistic Producer of The Paprika Festival from 2015-2017; a youth-led theatre festival and year-round training/mentorship program for young artists. I created the inaugural Acting program at Centauri Arts . I received OAC funding to co-run a theatre program for students at Heydon Park Secondary School; a school that aims to be the preferred secondary school in Toronto in vocational and experiential learning for female students. In 2012 I was a facilitator for the inaugural theatre program for BluePrints for Life, one of the worlds leading companies using hip-hop as both a community development tool and as a model for alternative education and healing. In 2014 I finished two internships with Canadian Stage: Artistic Intern and the inaugural Dramaturgical Internship.

I am currently the administrative artistic associate to Ravi Jain. I also won the Professional Theatre Training Program grant to assistant direct Ravi on Animal Farm at Soulpepper (see my blog posts here, here and here).
